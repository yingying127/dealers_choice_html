Burj Khalifa

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Burj Khalifa is 163 stories has a height of 2,722 feet or 828 meters. It was completed in 2009, and has been the tallest building ever since. It was designed based on the governments decision to diversify from an oil-based economy, and for Dubai to gain international recognition.

Merdeka 118

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Merdeka 118 is 118 stories and has a height of 2,227 feet or 678.9 meters. It is still under construction and expected to finish by 2022. This building will be the first in Malaysia to receive a triple platinum rating from the Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED).

Shanghai Tower

Shanghai, China

Shanghai Tower is 128 stories and has a height of 2,073 feet or 632 meters. It was completed in 2014, and its observation deck was completed in 2016. This building is designed to capture rainwater for internal use, and to recycle a portion of its wastewater.
